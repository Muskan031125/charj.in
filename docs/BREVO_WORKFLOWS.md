# Brevo Workflow Blueprint

## Contact attributes

- FIRSTNAME
- MOBILE
- CITY
- LEAD_TYPE
- VEHICLE_NAME
- VEHICLE_CATEGORY
- BUDGET
- PURCHASE_TIMELINE
- FINANCE_REQUIRED
- CHARGING_REQUIRED
- SOURCE_URL

## Lists

- EV Buyers
- Scooter Buyers
- Car Buyers
- Commercial EV Buyers
- Fleet Leads
- Charger Installation Leads
- Dealer Partners
- Newsletter Subscribers

## Workflow: New EV Buyer Lead

Trigger: Contact added to EV Buyers list

1. Immediate confirmation email
2. 10 minutes: buyer guide
3. 1 day: comparison suggestions
4. 2 days: finance and EMI guide
5. 3 days: charging guide
6. 5 days: final callback CTA

## Workflow: Fleet Lead

Trigger: Contact added to Fleet Leads list

1. Immediate confirmation email
2. Alert admin and sales team
3. Send fleet ROI calculator
4. Send charging setup checklist
5. Follow-up reminder after 2 days

## Workflow: Charger Installation Lead

Trigger: Contact added to Charger Installation Leads list

1. Confirmation email
2. Home/society charger guide
3. Site readiness checklist
4. Partner callback reminder
